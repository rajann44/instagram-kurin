About Clean Feed Launcher

Clean Feed Launcher is a simple shortcut page that opens Instagram's official Following and Favorites feed variants.

What it does

It gives one-tap access to: Following and Favorites. No account data is collected or proxied by this site.

Trust and ownership

This site is an independent tool and is not affiliated with Instagram or Meta. Platform behavior and feed ordering are controlled by Instagram.
