Instagram Following vs Favorites

Both are cleaner than the default home feed. Use Following for all accounts you follow, and Favorites for your top-priority list.

Following feed

Shows posts from accounts you follow. It is usually closer to chronological order than the main algorithmic home feed.

Open Following

Favorites feed

Shows posts from accounts marked as Favorites. Good for people you never want to miss.
